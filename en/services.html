Structural Design & Calculations

Our structural engineering services ensure your building is safe, efficient, and compliant with all relevant building codes and standards.

What We Deliver:

  • Notes de Calcul - Comprehensive calculation reports in French/English
  • Foundation Design - Optimal foundation solutions for your soil conditions
  • Structural Analysis - Advanced computer modeling and analysis
  • Reinforcement Details - Detailed rebar drawings and specifications
  • Load Calculations - Dead, live, wind, and seismic load analysis
  • Material Specifications - Concrete grades, steel specifications

Standards We Follow:

  • Eurocode standards (EC2, EC8)
  • Local Niger building codes
  • French DTU standards
  • International seismic codes

Project Types

  • Commercial buildings

  • Healthcare buildings

BIM & Revit Modeling

Advanced Building Information Modeling (BIM) using Autodesk Revit for accurate 3D visualization, coordination, and documentation.

BIM Services:

  • 3D Modeling - Detailed architectural and structural models
  • Clash Detection - Identify conflicts before construction
  • Quantity Takeoffs - Accurate material quantities and costs
  • 4D Scheduling - Time-based construction sequencing
  • Collaboration - Multi-disciplinary coordination platform
  • Documentation - Automated drawings and schedules

Software Expertise:

  • Autodesk Revit (Architecture & Structure)
  • AutoCAD (2D drafting)
  • Robot Structural Analysis
  • Navisworks (coordination)
  • BIM 360 (collaboration)

  • Reduced errors and rework
  • Better visualization for clients
