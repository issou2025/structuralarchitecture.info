Meet Issoufou Abdou Chefou

Licensed Civil Engineer & BIM Specialist

With over 5 years of dedicated experience in civil engineering and structural design, I am passionate about creating safe, efficient, and sustainable building solutions for clients across Niger and West Africa.

My journey in engineering began with a strong foundation in civil engineering principles, which I've continuously expanded through practical experience and modern technology adoption. Today, I specialize in combining traditional engineering excellence with cutting-edge BIM technology to deliver superior results.

Professional Expertise

My core expertise spans several critical areas of civil engineering:

  • Structural Analysis & Design: Comprehensive structural calculations, foundation design, and reinforcement detailing using both manual calculations and advanced software
  • BIM & 3D Modeling: Proficient in Autodesk Revit for creating detailed architectural and structural models, enabling better visualization and coordination
  • Construction Supervision: Hands-on experience in project management, quality control, and site supervision ensuring projects meet specifications and timelines
  • Building Code Compliance: In-depth knowledge of local and international building codes, ensuring all designs meet safety and regulatory requirements

Why Choose Me

What sets me apart is my commitment to understanding the unique challenges of building in West Africa's climate and conditions, combined with modern engineering practices:

  • Local Expertise: Deep understanding of Niger's soil conditions, climate considerations, and construction practices
  • Modern Technology: Leveraging BIM and advanced analysis software for accuracy and efficiency
  • Bilingual Communication: Fluent in both French and English, facilitating clear communication with diverse clients
  • Practical Approach: Focus on practical, cost-effective solutions that can be successfully implemented
