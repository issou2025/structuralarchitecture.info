Technical Questions

Structural Engineering

What structural engineering services do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive structural engineering services including:

  • Structural analysis and design calculations
  • Foundation design and soil analysis
  • Reinforced concrete and steel structure design
  • Load calculations and safety assessments
  • Structural modifications and retrofitting
  • Code compliance verification

What building codes and standards do you follow?

+

We follow internationally recognized building codes and standards including:

  • Eurocode design standards (EC0, EC1, EC2, EC8)
  • French DTU (Documents Techniques Unifi√©s) standards
  • West African regional building codes
  • Niger national construction regulations
  • International seismic design standards

Do you provide soil analysis and foundation recommendations?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive foundation engineering services including:

  • Soil bearing capacity analysis
  • Foundation type recommendations (shallow vs. deep foundations)
  • Settlement analysis and recommendations
  • Groundwater level considerations
  • Foundation design calculations and drawings

We work with certified geotechnical testing labs for soil investigations when required.

Building Plans & Design

What's included in your building plan packages?

+

Our complete building plan packages include:

  • Architectural Drawings: Site plan, floor plans, elevations, sections
  • Structural Drawings: Foundation plan, framing plan, reinforcement details
  • Engineering Calculations: Structural analysis, load calculations
  • Specifications: Material lists, construction specifications
  • 3D Visualization: BIM models and renderings
  • Documentation: Project guidelines and support materials

Can you modify existing building plans?

+

Yes, we offer plan modification services including:

  • Room layout changes and additions
  • Structural modifications and reinforcement
  • Adapting plans to specific site conditions
  • Updating plans to current building codes
  • Customizing plans for client preferences

Minor modifications are often included free of charge, while major changes may require additional fees.

Do you use BIM (Building Information Modeling)?

+

Yes, we are proficient in BIM technology using Autodesk Revit for:

  • 3D architectural and structural modeling
  • Clash detection and coordination
  • Quantity takeoffs and material lists
  • 4D construction sequencing
  • Enhanced visualization for clients
  • Improved accuracy and reduced errors

BIM models help clients better understand their projects and facilitate smoother construction.

Commercial Questions

Pricing & Payment

How much do building plans cost?

+

Building plan costs vary based on several factors:

  • Project size and complexity
  • Type of building (residential, commercial, specialized)
  • Level of detail required
  • Custom modifications needed

We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and estimate.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Bank transfers (local and international)
  • Mobile money (Orange Money, Airtel Money)
  • Cash payments (for local clients)
  • Installment payment plans (for larger projects)

Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion of plans.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

+

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties:

  • 6-month warranty on all building plans and calculations
  • Free revisions for any errors or omissions
  • Ongoing support during construction phase
  • Professional liability insurance coverage

Our warranty covers the accuracy and completeness of all engineering work.

Process & Timeline

How long does it take to complete building plans?

+

Timeline depends on project complexity:

  • Standard residential plans: 5-10 business days
  • Custom residential designs: 2-3 weeks
  • Commercial buildings: 3-6 weeks
  • Complex/specialized structures: 4-8 weeks

We provide detailed project schedules and keep you updated throughout the design process.

Do you help with building permit applications?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive permit support:

  • Permit-ready drawings and documentation
  • Guidance on local permit requirements
  • Assistance with permit application preparation
  • Responding to municipal review comments
  • Liaison with local authorities when needed

Our plans are designed to meet all local building code requirements for smooth permit approval.

What information do you need to start a project?

+

To begin your project, we typically need:

  • Site information: Plot dimensions, location, soil conditions
  • Project requirements: Number of rooms, intended use, budget
  • Design preferences: Architectural style, special features
  • Timeline expectations: When you plan to start construction
  • Local requirements: Any specific municipal requirements

We'll guide you through gathering all necessary information during our initial consultation.
